Instructions

Using the Road Trip Generator is simple and fun! Here's how:

  1. Click the "Next" button to get a random road trip instruction.
  2. Follow the instruction as best as you can.
  3. If you want a new instruction, click "Next" again.
  4. Keep going until you're ready to head home or stop for the day.

This tool is designed for spontaneity and exploration. Use it to make your road trips more exciting and adventurous!
